How can I help stop pollution?



You can help stop pollution by recycling any items from the list (Yellow bin)



  • Aluminium , steel tins and cans.
  • Areosol cans (Needs to be empty)
  • Glass bottles and jars (Leave lids on)
  • Aluminium foil (Compress to a smaller shape to save space)
  • Plastic bottles including soft-drinks, water bottles, juice and milk. (Leave lids on)


    • Most 150ML - 3L drink containers are eligable for a 10c refund at a NSW return point.

    You should be able to find if your item is eligable to get a 10c refund by checking at the back of the bottle.
