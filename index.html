Pollution

What is Pollution?

Pollution is a greenhouse gas that is produced by cars, buses, planes and any fossil fuel products. Pollution can make the
Earth become warmer by producing alot of excess heat by trapping the heat inside the Earth.



How does it impact?

Currently pollution is impacting 99% of the worlds population (8 Billion). However, it impacts the most with people with asthma, and lung conditions.

How can I help?

You can help by recycling your plastic bottles and cans into the yellow bin. Most of the aluminium cans and bottles can be refunded at your nearest NSW return point for 10c.
